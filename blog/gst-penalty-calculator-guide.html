What is GST Penalty?

GST penalties are monetary charges imposed on businesses for non-compliance with GST regulations, including late filing of returns, delayed tax payments, incorrect information, or failure to register under GST.

```

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: GST penalties consist of two components:

  • Late Fee: Fixed amount per day of delay
  • Interest: Percentage of unpaid tax amount

Legal Provisions

GST penalties are governed by:

  • Section 47: Late fee for delayed filing
  • Section 50: Interest on delayed tax payment
  • Section 122-138: Other penalties for various violations
```

How to Calculate GST Late Fee

Formula for Late Fee

Late Fee = Number of Days √ó Daily Rate

  • Daily Rate (NIL return): ‚Çπ40 per day (‚Çπ20 CGST + ‚Çπ20 SGST)

Late fee applies to:

Days of delay are counted from:

How to Calculate GST Interest

```

Interest Rate

18% per annum on the outstanding tax amount.

Formula for Interest

Interest = (Tax Amount √ó 18% √ó Number of Days) √∑ 365

Key Points

  • Interest is calculated on unpaid CGST, SGST, and IGST separately
  • Charged from the day after due date until payment
  • No maximum cap on interest amount
  • Compounds if not paid (interest on interest)

Frequently Asked Questions

```

What is the maximum GST late fee?

The maximum late fee is capped at ‚Çπ5,000 per return (‚Çπ2,500 CGST + ‚Çπ2,500 SGST). However, there is no cap on interest charges.

Can GST penalties be waived?

The government occasionally announces amnesty schemes where late fees may be waived or reduced. Interest charges are typically not waived except in special circumstances. Check the GST portal for current schemes.

What happens if I don't pay GST penalties?

Non-payment of penalties can lead to:

  • GST registration suspension or cancellation
  • Inability to file future returns
  • Additional penalties and prosecution
  • Bank account or property attachment

Is late fee different for composition dealers?

Yes, composition dealers filing GSTR-4 quarterly also face ‚Çπ50/day (‚Çπ25 CGST + ‚Çπ25 SGST) late fee, capped at ‚Çπ5,000. The same 18% interest applies on delayed tax payments.

How is interest calculated if I file on time but pay tax late?

Even if you file your return on time, interest at 18% per annum is charged from the day after the due date until the date of actual tax payment.

Can I claim ITC on penalties paid?

No, Input Tax Credit cannot be claimed on late fees or interest paid under GST. These are non-creditable expenses as per Section 17(5).
