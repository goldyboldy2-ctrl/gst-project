Interest on Late Payment of Tax

Besides late fees for delayed filing, you also pay interest on unpaid tax. This is calculated separately.

Interest Rate

  • Calculated on: Tax amount (not on total invoice value)

Special Cases & Exceptions

GSTR-1 Has No Interest

This is a common confusion point. GSTR-1 attracts late fees but NO interest because:

  • GSTR-1 only reports sales (outward supplies)
  • Actual tax payment happens through GSTR-3B
  • Since no tax payment in GSTR-1, no interest applies
  • You only pay late fees for delayed filing

GSTR-4 (Composition Dealers)

Composition scheme dealers have a fixed cap of ‚Çπ2,000 for GSTR-4 late filing, regardless of turnover. This is because composition dealers are by definition small businesses (turnover < ‚Çπ1.5 Cr).
