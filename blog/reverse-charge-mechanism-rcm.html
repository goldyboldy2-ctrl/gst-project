Quick Summary

RCM = Buyer pays GST instead of seller. Applies to specific goods/services and unregistered suppliers.

What is RCM?

In Reverse Charge Mechanism, the recipient (buyer) pays GST instead of the supplier (seller).

Normally: Seller collects GST ‚Üí Pays to government

Under RCM: Buyer pays GST directly to government

When RCM Applies

1. Unregistered Supplier

Registered person buying from unregistered supplier (suspended till further notice)

2. Specific Services

  • GTA (Goods Transport Agency) services
  • Legal services from advocates
  • Security/manpower services from individuals
  • Services from director to company

3. Specific Goods

  • Cashew nuts (not shelled/peeled)
  • Bidi wrapper leaves
  • Tobacco leaves
  • Silk yarn

How to Comply

  1. Calculate tax: Apply GST rate on invoice value
  2. Pay tax: Use cash (ITC cannot be used)
  3. Claim ITC: Can claim ITC in same/next month
  4. Report: Show in Table 3.1(d) and 4A(3) of GSTR-3B

Important Points

  • Cannot use ITC for payment (must pay cash)
  • Can claim ITC after payment
  • Invoice must mention "Tax payable under RCM"
  • Supplier cannot charge GST on RCM supplies
