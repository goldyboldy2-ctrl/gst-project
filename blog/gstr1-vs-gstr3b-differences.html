Quick Summary

GSTR-1 = Sales (due 11th). GSTR-3B = Summary + payment (due 20th). File GSTR-1 first.

GSTR-1: Sales Return

Purpose: Report all sales

Due: 11th of next month

Contains: Invoice details

GSTR-3B: Summary Return

Purpose: Summary + pay tax

Due: 20th of next month

Contains: Summary values
