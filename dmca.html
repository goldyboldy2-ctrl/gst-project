1. Introduction

MyGSTIndia respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects our users to do the same. This policy outlines our procedure for addressing copyright infringement claims under the Indian Copyright Act, 1957 and the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

2. Copyright Protection

All original content on MyGSTIndia, including but not limited to:

  • Calculator tools and algorithms
  • Blog articles and guides
  • HSN database compilation
  • Website design and graphics
  • Original text and commentary

...is protected by copyright and may not be reproduced without permission.

3. Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe that content on our website infringes your copyright, please send a written notice to our designated agent containing:

Required Information:

  1. Identification of copyrighted work: Describe the work you believe has been infringed
  2. Location of infringing material: Provide the specific URL or location on our site
  3. Your contact information: Name, address, telephone number, and email
  4. Good faith statement: A statement that you believe in good faith that the use is not authorized
  5. Accuracy statement: A statement that the information in your notice is accurate
  6. Authorization: Your physical or electronic signature
  7. Authority statement: A statement that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner

5. Our Response Process

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, we will:

  1. Review the claim for completeness and validity
  2. Remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing material (if valid)
  3. Notify the user who posted the content (if applicable)
  4. Respond to the complainant within 7-14 business days

6. Counter-Notice Procedure

If you believe content was removed in error, you may file a counter-notice containing:

  • Your contact information
  • Identification of the removed material and its former location
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake
  • Consent to jurisdiction of courts in India
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Send counter-notices to: mygstconnect@gmail.com

7. Repeat Infringer Policy

We will terminate access for users who are repeat copyright infringers. A repeat infringer is someone who:

  • Has been notified of infringing activity more than twice
  • Has failed to remove or disable infringing content after notice

8. Fair Use Consideration

We respect the doctrine of fair use and will consider whether material qualifies as fair use before removing content. Fair use may include:

  • Brief quotations for commentary or criticism
  • Educational use with proper attribution
  • News reporting
  • Transformative works

9. Content We Create

Most content on MyGSTIndia is:

  • Original content created by us
  • Based on publicly available information (GST laws, rates)
  • Not copyrighted where it consists purely of factual data (like HSN codes which are public domain)

Our unique compilation, presentation, and commentary IS protected by copyright.

10. External Content

If you believe we've linked to infringing external content, please note:

  • We do not host third-party content
  • We are not responsible for content on external websites
  • Contact the host of the infringing content directly

However, we will remove links to clearly infringing content if notified.

11. Good Faith Requirement

We expect all DMCA notices to be submitted in good faith. Misuse of the DMCA process may result in:

  • Legal liability under Section 512(f)
  • Dismissal of future claims
  • Potential monetary damages

12. Limitations

MyGSTIndia is not responsible for:

  • Copyright infringement by users on external platforms
  • Third-party websites that copy our content
  • Content we link to but do not host
