About MyGSTIndia

MyGSTIndia is a GST rate information portal providing compiled reference data from public notifications issued by the Government of India.

Our Purpose

We aim to simplify access to GST rate information by organizing publicly available data into an easy-to-search format. Our platform helps businesses, accountants, and individuals quickly find GST rates and HSN codes for common goods and services.

What We Provide

  • Compiled GST rate reference based on latest notifications
  • HSN code information for 500+ commonly searched items
  • Category-wise organization of GST data
  • GST calculation tools and resources

Data Sources

All information on this portal is compiled from publicly available sources including:

  • GST Rate Notifications (Central Tax)
  • CBIC (Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs) circulars
  • Public tariff publications from gst.gov.in

Important Note

MyGSTIndia is an informational portal and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Government of India, CBIC, or any government body. The information provided is for general reference purposes only and does not constitute legal or tax advice.

GST rates are subject to change through government notifications and may vary based on specific product classifications, use cases, and other factors. Users are strongly advised to verify all information with official sources at gst.gov.in or consult a qualified Chartered Accountant before making business decisions.
