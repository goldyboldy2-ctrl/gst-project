TL;DR: We don't collect your personal data. We use Google Analytics to see how people use our site. Your calculations stay on your device. We don't sell anything to anyone.

1. Introduction

MyGSTIndia ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website mygstindia.in.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

```

2.1 Personal Information

We DO NOT collect personal information. Our tools and calculators work entirely in your browser. We do not:

  • Ask for your name, email, or phone number
  • Require registration or login
  • Store your calculations or data
  • Collect payment information (everything is free)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, desktop)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Geographic location (country/city level only)

This data is anonymized and aggregated. We cannot identify individual users.

```

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Understand which tools are most useful
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Understand what content to create

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use data for advertising
  • Share data with anyone (except Google Analytics)
  • Track you across other websites

4. Cookies and Tracking

```

4.1 Google Analytics Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to:

  • Distinguish unique visitors
  • Track session duration
  • Remember user preferences (like returning visitor status)

These cookies do not collect personal information and are anonymized.

4.2 How to Opt-Out

You can opt-out of Google Analytics by:

  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser settings
  • Using browser extensions that block analytics
```

5. Your Calculations Are Private

All calculations happen in your browser. This means:

  • Your GST calculations never leave your device
  • We don't see what amounts you enter
  • Your data is not stored on our servers
  • You can use our tools offline (once loaded)

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
