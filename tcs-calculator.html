üìã TCS Under GST - Key Points

  • Who: E-commerce operators (marketplace model)
  • Rate: 1% of net taxable value (0.5% CGST + 0.5% SGST or 1% IGST)
  • Collected from: Payment to supplier (not buyer)
  • Net Value: Gross sales - Returns - GST
  • Deposit: By 10th of next month
  • Form: File GSTR-8 monthly
  • Certificate: GSTR-8A issued to supplier
  • Supplier Claims: TCS credit in GSTR-3B
