Section 17(5) - Blocked ITC

ITC is NOT available on:

  • Motor vehicles (except for specific business use)
  • Food, beverages, and outdoor catering
  • Membership of clubs, health & fitness centers
  • Life insurance and pension fund management
  • Goods/services for personal consumption
  • Works contract for construction of immovable property (except plant & machinery)

Note: There are specific exceptions. Always verify eligibility with your CA based on your actual use case.
